Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock - Rules

Click one of the images to choose your option! Whoever has more round wins after 3 rounds is the winner!

Match-ups

  • Rock - Beats Scissors and Lizard, Loses to Paper and Spock
  • Paper - Beats Rock and Spock, Loses to Lizard and Scissors
  • Scissors - Beats Paper and Lizard, Loses to Rock and Spock
  • Lizard - Beats Spock and Paper, Loses to Scissors and Rock
  • Spock - Beats Rock and Scissors, Loses to Lizard and Paper
